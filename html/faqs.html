Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

Who can rent a car?

The car with driver can be rented by anyone above the age of 16. However, in case of self-drive, minimum age for hiring a car is 18 years. If you are a resident of Pakistan, you need to have a valid driving license along with CNIC. If you are on a tourist visa then you need to have a valid home country license OR an international driving license and valid passport with visa stamp.

Can I rent a car with and without driver both?

Yes, you can book any car available on our website with or without driver both

Are there any delivery and collection charges?

Delivery charges are Rs.1,000/- however, no collection charges are applied.

How can I pay the rent?

We accept cash and bank transfers at the moment and an option for online payment will be added soon.

What are the delivery & collection timings?

The car delivery and collection time is from 8:00am to 11:30pm in case of Self-Drive. However, in case of bookings with driver, we are available 24/7.

Do I need to pay any security deposit?

No, in case of any damage the amount will be charged after returning the vehicle.

What is your fuel policy?

We rent all our cars with full or almost fuel tank. However, in case the journey is long and the car needs more fuel, the customer will have to pay for the refill.

Which cities are you serving?

Currently we are serving customers only in Karachi.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

Refunds will be done only through the Original Mode of Payment which can take up to 3 to 5 working days. Cancellation is free for 24 or more hours prior to booking date. The cancellation charges would be 50% of the booking amount for all cars except luxury cars. The cancellation fee for luxury cars is 10% of the booking amount.
